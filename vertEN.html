GREEN'

The green consists of making figures on a ramp called a halfpipe , U- shaped , the walls of which end vertically (from 30 to 70 cm depending on the ramps).

Even if it is undoubtedly the most spectacular practice of BMX, it is perhaps the least practiced nowadays because it is one of the most demanding disciplines, requiring years of training but above all very heavy infrastructures.

A typical ramp generally measure 3.50 m (instead of 4 m today) high and the best get off the ground 3 m or 4.50 m above the ramp.
