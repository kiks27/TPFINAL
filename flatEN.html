FLAT

The flat is a discipline that consists of doing figures, or sequences of figures, exclusively on flat ground, often balanced on a single wheel or using pegs .

The flat is changing very rapidly in recent years and is more an art form than a sport; it is a very demanding discipline that can be compared to dance, similar to breakdance or hip-hop dance . In fact, in the competitions (more often called contests or jams ), the originality and the style of the practitioners are largely put forward to the detriment of the purely sporting and competitive side. The originality of the tricks makes the difference in competition.

For the flat , we use a shorter and lighter bike, which allows better handling. Many flatlanders use only the front brake or no brake at all, which makes the practice more difficult but also more impressive and much smoother.
